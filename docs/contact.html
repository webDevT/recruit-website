Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to go live?
Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur, On average within 1 week. Depends on how quickly you provide content and approve the design.
Do I need technical knowledge?
No. The CMS is intuitive and easy to use. We provide documentation and training. Need extra help? We offer hands-on support.
Can I use my own domain?
Yes! The website runs under your own domain (e.g., careers.yourcompany.com or jobs.yourcompany.com). We help with DNS setup.
Can I customize the design?
Absolutely. Standard templates can be adjusted with our hands-on help service. Want something completely unique? We offer full custom design - request a quote.
Which ATS systems do you support?
We support 15+ ATS systems including Otys, Carerix, Bullhorn, Recruitee, and more. Check our integrations page or ask us.
What's the difference between annual and monthly contracts?
Annual contracts are €100/month (€1,200/year), monthly contracts are €125/month. Both include everything - the difference is just commitment period.
What if I want to cancel?
No problem. Cancel with 30 days notice. Your content and data remain yours. We'll help with export if needed.
Is hosting and maintenance included?
Yes, everything is included: hosting under your domain, SSL certificate, backups, security updates, and maintenance. No extra costs.
